After Tu Youyou won the Nobel Prize in Medicine, he deserved the award again

Category: Current Affairs Focus    release time: 2019-10-25    Author: admin
  Share to:   
QR code sharing

Tu Youyou has another good news! After winning the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for his contribution to “discovering new treatments for malaria” in 2015, Tu Youyou recently won the 2019 UNESCO-Equatorial Guinea International Life Science Research Award for research on parasitic diseases. . The new antimalarial drug artemisinin she discovered cured many Chinese patients in the 1980s. The World Health Organization recommends the use of artemisinin-based combination therapy as the global first-line antimalarial treatment program, saving millions of lives, reducing the mortality rate of malaria in Africa by 66%, and the mortality rate of malaria among children under 5 by 71%. Such an achievement deserves the award!



In addition to re-entering the public view due to medical achievements, Tu Youyou's benevolence as a doctor who cares about public health is also moving. Recently, he also sent a congratulatory letter to the first Tencent Medical ME Conference to be held in Beijing, and carried the banner of medical science popularization. She said, "There are many medical disciplines, and they are all asking questions and seeking answers to life. They all hope that humans can suffer less from illness and improve the quality of human life... And medical workers not only need to concentrate on researching topics in their own fields, they should also Undertake the responsibility of popularizing accurate and authoritative medical knowledge to the public. Life and health are related to the common destiny of mankind. The joint attention of the medical community and enterprises on medical science conforms to this development trend."


It is reported that the Tencent Medical ME Conference held in Beijing on November 4 is a medical event jointly created by Tencent and its subsidiary Tencent Medical Classic.. General Medicine Journal "New England Journal of Medicine", aiming to build a cross-cognition for the public The medical science popularization bridge of the gap narrows the distance between the public and pioneer medicine and medical science popularization.


The first Tencent Medical ME Conference will invite global medical leaders to focus on cancer, depression, AIDS and other disease topics that are of concern, show cutting-edge medical breakthroughs, and lead the audience to explore the mysteries of life.

A short documentary by the BBC spoke highly of Tu Youyou, “If you use the number of lives saved to measure the degree of greatness, then Tu Youyou is undoubtedly one of the great scientists in history!”

 "When we mention outstanding scientific figures, there is no doubt that Tu Youyou will be among them."
