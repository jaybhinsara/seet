The directional drilling with a depth of 3,353 meters penetrates the coal seam over a long distance, creating a new world record

Category: Current Affairs Focus    release time: 2019-10-26    Author: admin
  Share to:   
QR code sharing

It was learned from China Coal Science Xiâ€™an Research Institute on October 22, 2019 that the institute applied self-developed high-power directional drilling technology and equipment to demonstrate drilling projects in the Baode Coal Mine of Shendong Coal Group. The main hole depth 3353 has been completed a few days ago. The ultra-long directional drilling along the coal seam with a length of 10 meters has once again created a new world record for downhole directional drilling in my country.


It is reported that the hospital started drilling and construction from the 27th joint of the air inlet main road in the fifth panel of the Baode coal mine. The drilling took 21 days to penetrate the working face of the second panel, and the opposite side of the third lower panel of the second panel has a large return air. The tunnel was successfully penetrated, the main hole depth was 3,353 meters, the total footage was 4428 meters, the diameter was 120 mm, the top and bottom were branched 13 times, and the target coordinate error was less than 0.15% through the tunnel. The current average daily gas drainage volume exceeded 5000 cubic meters. Meter.


"The drilling broke the world record of the 2570-meter ultra-long directional drilling hole we created in Baode Mine at the beginning of 2019. This drilling project achieved a significant increase in the drilling depth and the realization of ultra-long-distance high-precision hits. , Mainly due to the development of the ultra-long directional hole sliding drilling drag reduction technology and the compound drilling trajectory control method in the coal mine, which greatly improved the drilling capacity and drilling efficiency of the downhole directional drilling; the ZDY15000LD independently developed by us was applied Directional drilling equipment, YHD3-3000 mud pulse wireless measurement while drilling system, large through-hole high-strength cableless directional drilling tool, realizes the stable transmission of wireless measurement while drilling pressure pulse signal at a hole depth of more than 3000 meters; in addition, we also Industrial application of the newly developed flushing fluid purification circulation system for underground coal mines has solved the problem of large water consumption and large sewage discharge in the construction of ultra-long holes, and realized the directional drilling flushing fluid from'open' to'closed' The cycle of leapfrogging has promoted the development of green drilling technology and equipment in coal mines." Xu Chao, site project manager of Xi'an Research Institute of Coal Science and Technology of China, introduced.


It is understood that the successful implementation of the 3353-meter ultra-long through directional hole along the coal seam is a new milestone in the development of downhole directional drilling technology and equipment, marking that my country has reached the international level in the field of downhole directional drilling.
