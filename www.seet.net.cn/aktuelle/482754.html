Siemens: Building a digital factory for the development of composite aerospace structures

Category: Current Affairs Focus    release time: 2019-12-06    Author: admin

China Aviation News
Building a Digital Factory for Composite Aerospace Structure Development-Interview with John O'Connor, Director of Fibersim Product and Aerospace Marketing, Siemens Industrial Software Co., Ltd. Composite material parts manufacturing is one of the specific applications of Siemens' digital factory program, demonstrating Siemens' industry...composite material technology capabilities. Such a composite material research and development system includes product design and process planning (PLM), manufacturing operations management (Manufacturing Operations Management), industrial automation system (SIMATIC), and motion control (SINUMERK), providing complete and powerful composites in the industry Material parts manufacturing solutions. Siemens PLM Software is an important participant in Siemens' global composite material digital plant technology program, and it is gradually being practiced in many aerospace companies around the world.

Original: http://www.chinacompositesexpo.com/cn/news-detail-247-1554.html
"China Aviation News 》:In the past twenty years, The application of composite materials has been significantly improved. What do you think is the main reason driving this trend?
John O’Connor :The global concern about environmental protection and fuel economy has caused people to pay more and more attention to execution efficiency in the aerospace and automotive industries. This also drives related companies to introduce some ** materials, including composite materials, into their products. Among them, composite materials with their remarkable specific strength and specific modulus make it possible for the company to design products that are more environmentally friendly and fuel-efficient. At the same time, this obvious trend also drives professional engineering software for composite materials. , Such as Fibersim, to help these units design and manufacture composite products that meet their needs.

"China Aviation News ":Composite materials and traditional materials What is the difference between finite element analysis and structural design?
John O’Connor :The finite element analysis and structural design of traditional materials such as metal materials are usually an isotropic material. Composite materials are not isotropic materials. Composite materials are extremely complex, and the indivisible plies are manufactured by methods similar to "assembly" and unique manufacturing processes, such as manual laying or automatic tape/fiber laying. This means that the performance of the composite structure is related to the type of material, geometry and manufacturing process. These must be considered in the finite element analysis and structural design of composite materials.
Without professional tools, it is usually a difficult task to transfer the complex layer properties of composite materials in structural design and finite element analysis. In most cases, although the understanding of material properties is very detailed, how to analyze the actual composite material structure is still a challenge. These challenges come from two aspects: First, the complex properties of composite materials in the structural design stage are difficult to achieve sufficient data sharing in the engineering stage, which makes it difficult to perform accurate finite element analysis. This also leads to approximation and uncertainty in the design phase. Secondly, the'theoretical fiber direction' defined in the design stage is usually inconsistent with the'actual fiber direction' in the production process. This means that the material properties of the composite base are usually not fully understood, because the orientation of the fiber is the key to determining its properties.
The key to solving the above two problems is to provide an appropriate definition of composite materials, which can comprehensively consider material types, design methods and manufacturing methods. This definition completely captures the detailed composite material design and can help define the detailed composite material finite element model, improving the analysis results of the finite element analysis engineer, reducing over-design and optimizing the entire structure more completely. Fibersim is an effective tool to provide this key capability.

"China Aviation News ":composite materials in the next 5 years What is the development trend in the field of design?
John O’Connor :Composite material design has now reached a certain degree of broader market maturity. At present, the composite materials we are facing include not only aerospace, but also more and more other industries such as automobiles. It is more clear that in the next 5 years and in the future, the amount of composite materials will still maintain a rapid growth trend.
In the field of design, there are two key aspects to get continue to develop. The first is to adopt an optimized method. In traditional metal material design, there are some simplified design methods that have been applied for many years, but for composite materials, these old design methods can no longer be used directly. Because the characteristics of composite materials are completely different from those of metal materials, it is necessary to adopt new and more optimized design methods to replace the old design methods. Secondly, these design methods need to consider manufacturing constraints at the same time, and can optimize the design for product performance and production efficiency. Siemens' composite material structure design and finite element analysis solutions can support these two aspects. At the same time, we are continuing to focus and develop new design capabilities to better support our customers and meet their needs in composite material innovation. .

"China Aviation News 》:Can you introduce Siemens What design and analysis solutions can PLM Software provide for composite materials?
John O’Connor :Siemens PLM Software's strategy is to provide customers with global composite materials design, analysis and manufacturing with **, open solutions and services. We provide open solutions that allow users to use design and analysis tools that suit their situation, such as CAD, PLM, and CAE tools. Siemens PLM Software's composite material solutions are mainly based on Fibersim as the core design tool, which has been verified by many global composite materials users in actual projects in the past 30 years. Fibersim provides design methods for different types of parts, manufacturing evaluation and can control costs and avoid errors, while providing a wide range of composite material manufacturing equipment on the market (automatic blanking machine, laser projector, automatic tape laying machine / automatic laying machine Etc.) data interface.
At the same time, Siemens PLM Software also provides the ability of linear and nonlinear finite element analysis of composite materials. Through our Laminate Composites products, users can optimize the size of composite materials and evaluate the mechanical properties of composite materials (allowable values, load Etc.), layup optimization and verification of the initial laminate layup. After acquiring LMS, Siemens PLM Software supplemented this capability through SAMCEF. SAMCEF has the industry's best linear and non-linear composite material analysis capabilities, and progressive failure analysis (such as damage, impact, interlayer) Damage, etc.), and the ability to analyze large-scale composite parts. Siemens PLM Software's all composite materials-related products can provide powerful and comprehensive composite material design and analysis capabilities on the market.

"China Aviation News ":What are the advantages of Siemens PLM Software's capabilities in composite materials compared with other composite solutions?
John O’Connor :We are in the global composite design And the field of analysis...with more than 20 years of status. Our ** technology has been fully verified by more than 400 aerospace companies and their composite materials projects around the world. The reason why Siemens PLM Software's composite material solution is so powerful is that Fibersim can perform accurate composite material manufacturability analysis. This unique ability provides the best tools in the market to predict the direction of the fiber, and at the same time provides accurate data for the finite element analysis and manufacturing stage. This advantage supports comprehensive fiber simulation, which in turn helps the composite material design team to carry out continuous innovation in composite materials. More than 20 years of practice has shown that Siemens PLM Software's composite materials products are the ** tools of the composite materials industry, meeting the ever-increasing new challenges of the global composite materials industry.
