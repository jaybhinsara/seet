Congratulations to our company for winning the bid for the multi-station winding machine project of Wuhan Hangda Aviation Technology Development Co., Ltd.

Category: Company headlines    Author：admin

Congratulations to our company for undertaking the repair and renovation project of Ø7m×30m autoclave for domestic aviation unit recently.

Wuhan Hangda Aviation Technology Development Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise mainly engaged in aircraft accessory maintenance, development, production, test equipment and airport refueling equipment R&D and production. It is one of the large-scale, large-scale, and wide-ranging aircraft accessory maintenance companies in China .

Hangda is located in Dongxihu District, Wuhan City, with a workshop area of ​​about 30,000 square meters in Wuhan, a 15-minute drive from Wuhan Tianhe Airport. The landing gear workshop in Anlu covers an area of ​​about 16,000 square meters, and the heat treatment plant covers an area of ​​about 15,000 square meters. Tianhe Airport is nearly 40 minutes away by car.

Hangda has obtained maintenance licenses from CAAC, FAA, EASA and some other countries or regions. At present, it has more than 5,000 accessory maintenance projects and special process capabilities and machining capabilities such as surface treatment, welding, NDT, and heat treatment.

Hangda has accumulated rich experience in the maintenance of pneumatic, hydraulic and electromechanical accessories, covering various series of Boeing, Airbus, DORNIER, CRJ, ERJ, and Russian-made aircraft. While providing maintenance services, Hangda also provides customers with maintenance exchange parts and aviation material leasing services, which can fully meet the various needs of customers.  

Hangda’s landing gear factory has complete landing gear overhaul and exchange parts support capabilities, and can provide a true one-stop service on the landing gear of mainstream models such as B737CL/NG, B757, B777, A320, and A330.

Hangda actively cooperates with OEMs in many aspects, and has become an authorized repair station for Eaton, Unison, Meggitt, and has obtained spare parts supply and technical support from OEMs such as Boeing, Honeywell, Hamilton Sundstrand, Parker, and Liebherr.

Hangda has established a relatively complete inventory management system and production management system to provide technical support for providing customers with fast service.
