Pioneer takes the lead, starting from the heart-Rubber and plastic equipment giant KraussMaffei** debuts at the China International Import Expo

Category: Industry News    release time: 2019-11-20    Author: admin
  Share to:   
QR code sharing

"The ingenuity technology originated from Germany, and the inheritance and development of 181 years have made KraussMaffei unique in the global and Chinese markets. Participating in the CIIE this time hopes to bring forward-looking concepts of technology to China, and Chinese partners embrace the future of digitalization and circular economy together" said Mr. Cui Xiaojun, CEO of KraussMaffei China. "



Ning Gaoning, Secretary of the Party Committee and Chairman of ChemChina, and leaders of China National Chemical Corporation visited the KraussMaffei booth. Dr. Michael Ruf, the chief operating officer of KraussMaffei, introduced the circular economy solution on site.


Circular economy contributes to a sustainable future
At this exhibition, KraussMaffei brought the EdelWeiss blending modification technology specially designed for circular economy and recycling applications, aiming to provide closed-loop solutions for the recycling of plastics. The rated output of its production line is as high as 6 tons/hour. Its main advantage is that it can modify the recycled waste plastics and transform them into high-quality, high-efficiency and high-stability end products, while improving cost-effectiveness, making recycled materials more attractive to recycling companies and plastic manufacturers.
 
Digital pioneers and smart device manufacturing empower the future of Industry 4.0
At the CIIE, KraussMaffei also introduced digital solutions such as DataXplorer, eService and eStore to help customers monitor production and greatly improve production efficiency.

The key to digitalization lies in the integration and integration of machine data. The DataXplorer provided by KraussMaffei to users is the central data collection point for all components in the production cell, including peripherals and automation. Users can collect all the information needed for production monitoring and production process control through it, and realize digitization.


Innovative design and applications make future life better
Combined with comprehensive expertise in the fields of injection molding, extrusion, reaction molding, digitalization and automation, KraussMaffei can provide users with unlimited possibilities for rubber and plastic processing. The exhibition site also demonstrated a variety of new product technologies and innovative market applications, including Colorform color molding, surface RTM surface resin transfer molding, Pultrusion pultrusion and other technologies, which are widely used in medicine, food, housing, transportation, and use. All areas closely related to people's daily lives.
 
The new product PXA Venture Edition all-electric injection molding machine has high performance, high flexibility and ductility, and has a wide range of applications. It provides unlimited possibilities for future upgrades and integration with Industry 4.0.
 
Sign the strategic cooperation framework memorandum on site
On November 6, the China International Import Expo was launched. At the booth, KraussMaffei signed an agreement of intent to purchase high-end injection molding equipment with Hubei Tianmen Urban Construction Investment Co., Ltd., and with the strong support of the Tianmen Municipal Government, they worked together to create The high-end injection mold industry platform facilitates the industrial upgrading and development of the molding industry chain centered on Tianmen, and forms strategic cooperation in the development of high-end automobile molds, new processes and new technologies.
 

 
In the afternoon of the same day, KraussMaffei, as a subsidiary of China National Chemical Corporation, also participated in the signing ceremony hosted by Ning Gaoning, Secretary of the Party Committee and Chairman of China National Chemical Corporation.
KraussMaffei and Beijing New Energy Automotive Technology Innovation Center Co., Ltd. and Beijing Carbon Fiber Engineering Technology Research Center signed a trilateral strategic cooperation framework memorandum. In the future, the three parties will cooperate on "composite material lightweight technology" and establish innovation The technology center builds a technical cooperation platform to promote the progress and application of lightweight technology for new energy vehicles.
 
KraussMaffei and China Resources Chemical Materials Technology Co., Ltd. signed a framework memorandum of strategic cooperation. The two parties will share experimental resources and research results on related topics in the future, industrialization technology research and forward-looking technology and product innovation research and development in all aspects. Intention to cooperate. Strive to use international composite material molding equipment and technology to produce green and environmentally friendly composite products with excellent performance.
 

KraussMaffei’s Global Operations Officer, Dr. Michael Ruf, signed the contract on behalf of the company and commented: “The achievement of these strategic cooperation is a manifestation of the spirit of the “New Era, Shared Future” theme of the CIIE, and also demonstrates the With a global view, Smartisan is confident of a win-win future with customers starting from the heart with German ingenuity and Chinese rhythm."


克劳斯玛菲– 先锋塑料
KraussMaffei is one of the global manufacturers of rubber and plastic production, processing machinery and systems. Our brand represents cutting-edge technology and has a history of 181 years. The services we provide cover all fields of injection molding, extrusion and reaction molding machinery and equipment and technologies. This makes KraussMaffei stand out in the industry. With our innovative capabilities in standardized and personalized products, processes, digital and service solutions, we ensure that our customers receive continuous added value throughout the value-added chain. Our wide range of products and services can provide services to customers in many industries, including automotive, packaging, medical, construction, and electronic and electrical products and household appliances manufacturers. KraussMaffei has approximately 5,500 employees worldwide, has more than 30 subsidiaries and more than 10 production plants, and has established partnerships with approximately 570 commercial and service companies to maintain close relationships with global customers. Since 1838, the headquarters has been in Munich.
