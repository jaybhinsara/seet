MIKROSAM launched the world’s largest fully automatic CHG/CNG cylinder production line

Category: Industry News    release time: 2019-11-21    Author: admin
  Share to:   
QR code sharing

  2019, in Prile, Macedonia In General City, Mikrosam Company solemnly launched a new fully automatic production line for the production of compressed hydrogen cylinders (CHG) and compressed natural gas cylinders (CNG) in the automotive industry in the world. The entire production line will include multiple multi-station filament winding machines (FW) integrated with Mikrosam's innovative fully automatic device, and the annual output of gas cylinders can reach more than 60,000. This contract with JSC DPO Plastic is the result of a two-year fiercely competitive design and bidding process.

       Mikrosam will report to JSC DPO Plastic provides a new and a ** multi-station filament winding machine for wet and prepreg filament winding. Its innovation is that it can wind glass and carbon fibers very quickly and accurately. These multi-station filament winding machines are designed to produce various gas cylinders ranging from 40 liters to 350 liters for storing CNG and CHG.


       except for Mikrosam. In addition to the automatic yarn cutting/continuation winding system, high-speed dual-drive trolley, and high-precision prepreg filament winding function, the winding equipment also has a technical tooling shaft specially designed for pressure vessels. This system can program control and continuously adjust the internal pressure in the cylinder liner during the winding process of each layer. In addition, in the wet winding process, the mixing of the resin, the transfer to the resin tank, and the impregnation of the resin on the fiber are all achieved excellent control. The integration of these features not only ensures a very fast speed, but also guarantees a consistently high winding quality, and each cylinder produced has a good material usage rate.

       Mikrosam's engineering technology Extend and expand to easily realize mass production by integrating multiple filament winding workstations, while also ensuring the high quality and high productivity of the entire production line. In order to achieve large-scale production and maximize equipment utilization, the production line is equipped with a complex dynamic load balancing system, which can ensure the simultaneous operation of four or more stations of the winding machine. This dynamic load balancing provides a higher overall utilization rate than simply placing many filament winding machines in the workshop. The design of Mikrosam equipment effectively improves the automation of multiple winding machines.

The robot-based handling unit controls and measures the weight of the bottle from the plastic inner liner to each stage after curing. The TCON system-the ** software developed by Mikrosam-collects the process parameter details of each step in the production process into the database, and can even collect from third-party supporting equipment (such as blow molding machines, test equipment, etc.) data. The software integrates data into the factory ERP to ensure complete traceability of the production process and improve quality, while at the same time providing users with unlimited reporting capabilities.
