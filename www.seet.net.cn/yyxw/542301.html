U.S. Hexcel Composites will merge with Woodward Corporation. The new company's market value is estimated to be approximately US$13.7 billion.

Category: Industry News    release time: 2020-01-15    Author: admin
  Share to:   
QR code sharing

January 12, Hexcel ) AndWoodwardannouncing that it has reached a final agreement. The two parties will establish a new company, Woodward Hexcel, through an all-share reciprocal merger. According to the closing price of the two parties on January 10, the transaction is worth about 6.43 billion U.S. dollars.

       Woodward is At present, one of the world’s largest suppliers of control systems and solutions with a long history and a large scale. The scope of services covers aviation, power generation, power distribution, processing industry, transportation industry, etc. The products are in aviation engines, gas turbines, internal combustion engines, steam Turbines, power system equipment, wind power generation equipment, etc. have applications.

       Hexcel Composite The company is a global...** structural materials company. It mainly develops and manufactures lightweight and high-performance composite materials, including carbon fibers, reinforced fabrics, prepregs, honeycomb cores, resin systems, adhesives and composite components, which are widely used in civil aircraft, aerospace, national defense and other fields.



       According to the agreement, The shareholders of Shi Fucai will redeem Woodward's common stock at a ratio of 1:0.625; and Woodward's shareholders will continue to hold the same number of original common stocks in the new company. After the merger is completed, the original shareholders of Woodward and Hexcel Composites will hold 55% and 45% of the shares in the new company, respectively. The transaction will increase Woodward's quarterly dividend per share to $0.28.

       the new company headquarters will Located in Fort Collins, Colorado, USA, it has more than 16,000 employees and has production operations in 14 countries on five continents, covering multiple terminal areas. According to calculations by Sina Finance, the market value of the new company after the merger will reach 13.7 billion U.S. dollars, making it one of the largest parts suppliers in the global aerospace field.
